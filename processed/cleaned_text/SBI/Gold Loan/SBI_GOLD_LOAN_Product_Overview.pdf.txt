SBI Gold Loan can be availed by pledge of gold ornaments including gold coins sold by Banks with
minimum paper work and low interest rate.
**Dial 1800-1234/1800 2100 for more information/ applying through Contact Centre.
Give a Missed Call on 7208933143 or SMS “GOLD” on 7208933145 to get a call back from our Contact
Centre**
Features
* MinimumLoan Amount : Rs.20,000/- (For Overdraft Product Min Amount: I5,00,000/-)
* Margin
+ Gold Loan (EMI based)**:** 28%
+ 12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan: 35%
+ Gold Loan Overdraft – 26%
* Security : Pledge of gold ornaments duly verified for quality & quantity.
* Others **:** Gold appraiser charges will be paid by the Applicant.
* Processing fee applicable is as under:
| Sr No | | Loan Amount | Processing Fee Amount |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | | I50,000/- to I2,00,000/- | 0.50% of the loan amount + applicable GST (Min: I500/- +GST |
| 2 | | Above I2.00 Lakhs upto I25.00 Lakhs | 0.30% of the loan amount + applicable GST (Min:
I1000/- +GST) |
| 3 | | Above I25.00 Lakhs | 0.25% of the loan amount + applicable GST (Min: I7,500/-+ applicable
GST Max: I15,000/-+ applicable GST) |
Eligibility
* **Age :** 18 years and above
* **Profession :** Any individual (singly or jointly) with Steady source of income including: Bank’s
Employees, Pensioners. (For Loan amount of more than Rs.2.5 Lacs, supporting income documents
are required).
Repayment
* Repayment Mode
+ **Gold Loan (EMI based)**: Repayment of Principal and Interest will commence from month following
the month of disbursement.
+ **12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan:**Interest and Principal on or before end of term.
+ **Gold Loan Overdraft**: Overdraft account with transaction facility and monthly interest is to be
served.
* Repayment Period
+ **Maximum :**
**Gold Loan (EMI based)****:** 36 months
**12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan**: 12 months ,
**Gold Loan Overdraft**: 36 Months
Documents Required

1. P Gold Loan Application Form
2. 2 Latest passport size photographs
2. For Existing to bank (ETB) customers having KYC compliant account where change in address or
other details to be updated:
1. P Gold Loan Application Form
2. Copy of only the relevant OVD for change in address or other details
3. 2 latest Passport size photographs
3. For Non-KYC Compliant NTB customers and New to Bank (NTB) customers
1. P Gold Loan Application Form
2. Copy of any OVDs as Proof of Identity and proof of address
3. Two Latest Passport Size Photographs.
(OVDs : Passport, Aadhar Card, Voter’s ID Card, Driving License and MGNREGA Job Card No.)

1. What are the purposes for which SBI Personal Gold Loan can be availed?
a) SBI Personal Gold Loan is a general-purpose Loan scheme available to individuals, against security
of gold ornaments & specially minted gold coins (max 50 grams) sold by Banks.
b) It can be taken for meeting expenses of sudden medical exigency, margin money for Home Loan
and/or Education Loan, travel plans including consumption purpose.
2. How much time Bank takes to sanction Personal Gold Loan?
Personal Gold Loan is normally sanctioned on the same day of application and loan amount is credited
to customer account.
3. I am not an existing customer of SBI, can I apply for Personal Gold Loan?
Yes, you can apply for Personal Gold Loan. Only thing is that you need is to open a Saving Account
with KYC documents with SBI where loan amount would be deposited
4. Who is eligible for SBI Personal Gold Loan?
nyone of 18 years and above age with steady source of income.
5. Is Submission of proof of income by Borrower necessary?
No, it is not necessary, but she/he should have the capacity to repay the loan.
6. What are the documents required for availing SBI Personal Gold Loan?
* Two Passport Size Photographs.
* Proof of identity with proof of Address (OVD)
7. What is the minimum & maximum loan amount that can be availed?
Minimum Loan amount is Rs. 20000/- and maximum is Rs. 1 Cr per individual.
8. What are the processing charges?
* **The product wise processing charges are as under:**
+ For 12 Months Bullet Repayment – 0.50% of loan amount, subject to minimum I 500 and maximum
I 10,000 + applicable GST.
+ SBI Personal Gold Loan (EMI) - 0.50% of loan amount subject to minimum I 500 + applicable GST.
+ SBI Liquid Gold Loan (overdraft): 0.50% of loan amount, subject to maximum I 10,000 + applicable
GST.
9. What is the repayment period of the loan?
* For SBI 12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan – 12 Months
* For SBI Personal Gold Loan - EMI Based – 36 Months
* For Gold Loan Overdraft – 36 Months
10. How can I repay my Personal Gold Loan?
Personal Gold Loan is available as Demand Loan and Bullet Repayment and Overdraft facility. You can
repay Personal Gold Loan through regular EMI and Interest every month.
* Demand loan is payable through monthly EMI.
* In Bullet Repayment Gold Loan, customers have the option of paying principal and interest on or
before maturity of loan.
* In Overdraft, monthly interest is to be served. At the end of OD tenor, principal along with accrued
interest to be paid for account closure.
11. What is the Interest ?
Interest rate as on 16.06.2026 is as under:
| Variants | ROI |
| --- | --- |
| 12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan | At Present – 9.15% (0.45% above 1Year MCLR). |
| Gold Loan on EMI | At Present – 10.10% (1.40% above 1Year MCLR). |
| Gold Loan Overdraft | At Present – 10.10% (1.00% above 1Year MCLR). |
Note – Interest is subject to change as per Bank’s policy.
12. How much loan amount is available per gram of Gold?
The amount of loan is decided on the basis of Gold purity i. e. on the basis of carat (22, 20, 18) and net
weight. Loans are not granted against the security of Primary Gold i. e., 24 carats gold bars and
biscuits.
13. How much margin is required to take SBI Personal Gold Loan?
Variant wise applicable margins are as under:
* Gold Loan (EMI based): 28%
* 12 Months Bullet Repayment Gold Loan: 35%
* Gold Loan Overdraft – 26%
* These margins are applicable irrespective of Loan amount.
14. Can I avail loan against Gold Coins sold by other Private/Nationalized Banks?
Yes, loans can be availed against the security of specially minted gold coins sold by Banks weighing
maximum up to 50 grams per customer.
15. If I have availed one SBI Personal Gold Loan, can I apply for second loan?
Yes, you can avail maximum 3 gold loans, including the existing one within bank’s prescribed ceiling of
Rs 50.00 lacs.
16. Whether NRI customer is eligible for SBI Personal Gold Loan?
Yes, NRI Customer is eligible for loan with his temporary local address.
17. Is there any pre-payment penalty on a SBI Personal Gold Loan?
No, there is no prepayment penalty on SBI Gold Loan.
18. Are there any foreclosure charges on a SBI Personal Gold Loan?
No. Loan can be closed at any point of time without any foreclosure charges.
19. Can I apply for SBI Personal gold loan at any branch?
Yes. The Loan can be applied for at any branch. The loan can also be applied through SBI App
“YONO”.
20. What are the methods by which my gold will be verified?
Following methods are used for assaying of gold collateral :
1. Touch stone method
2. Nitric acid test
3. Specific gravity test
4. Gold Assaying machine
21. How net weight of Gold collateral is determined?
Answer: The net weight of gold content in collateral is arrived at by excluding all extraneous matters
such as wax, string, fastening, precious stones etc. on a liberal scale from gross weight of the Gold
collateral during appraisal of Gold for purity and weight.
22. What is the price used to value Gold collateral?
Answer: To value gold collateral, market price as determined by the bank on daily basis is used.
