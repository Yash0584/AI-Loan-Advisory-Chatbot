**Marginal Cost of Funds based Lending (MCLR) w.e.f. 01.11.2016**
Where more than 50% of NMI is coming from Salary Income
| Loan Amount | Interest |
| --- | --- |
| Upto Rs. 1 crore | 1.45% + 1 yr MCLR |
| Above Rs. 1 cr & upto Rs. 2 crs. | 2.10% + 1 yr MCLR |
| Above Rs. 2 crs. & upto Rs. 7.50 crs. | 2.50% + 1 yr MCLR |
Where more than 50% of NMI is coming from Business/Profession or
rental Income:
| Loan Amount | Interest |
| --- | --- |
| Upto Rs. 1 crore | 2.10% + 1 yr MCLR |
| Above Rs. 1 cr & upto Rs. 2 crs. | 2.60% + 1 yr MCLR |
| Above Rs. 2 crs. & upto Rs. 7.50 crs. | 3.00% + 1 yr MCLR |
(Interest rate will be reset at the end of every year from the date of first disbursement on the basis of
prevailing one year MCLR as on the date of reset.)
